 Global production of plastic

Plastic was a great solution in the food packaging industry, first they had to use glass bottles for transporting drinks but when they created plastic bottles this had an huge impact on the production process, since it was way cheaper and faster in production. Back then they didn't know that plastic would became such a problem. In 1950 the world produced only 2 million tonnes of plastic a year. Since then, annual production has increased nearly 200-fold, reaching 381 million tonnes in 2015.

 Where does it come from?

Where is this plastic coming from and how does it end up in the ocean? Most of the plastic today is used for packaging. Have you ever bought something that wasn't wrapped in plastic? When we go to the store we usually buy some fruits or bread, all those foods are wrapped in plastic. And for how long do we use this plastic? Once maybe twice... This is called the "singe use plastics". They are the worst because we use them once and then throw them away, often in a trash bin on our way home or even worse they end up laying on the streets.
